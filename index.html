My Datastory

Walking with Apple Music

The data collected consists of song names, plays and skips, time, and level of satisfaction of songs I listened to over the span of a week. The data visualization shows the paths taken to and from class, the level of satisfaction, my mood, and the approximate location the song was listened to based on timestamps.

Data Brokerage

Data brokerage is an ongoing practice that is defined as the collection of data and sold to organizations. In the accompanying video, this concept is further explained along with examples and current legislation on the practice. Just you can imagine how much data is collected day to day, Apple Music keeps track of songs listened to, which ones are liked, and which ones are skipped. They use this data to then serve you songs of the like and create a better listening experience.

Website Design

Throughout the semester I learned the complex nature of HTML and CSS Grid. From inline elements and block elements to the design of stylesheets. I took these lessons and used them to create this website to tell my datastory. Through the use of div's, headers, classes, and css it has allowed me to create a responsive website for various platforms. The design began with a mobile design and then on to a typical website design.
